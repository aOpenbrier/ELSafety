The Sternfels Prize for Drug Safety Discoveries

The Sternfels Prize recognizes research to reduce the risk associated with the real-world use of pharmaceuticals. The purpose of the prize is to incentivize pharmacologists, pharmacists, medical practitioners, academics, and students to think about ways to prevent the side effects that patients face due to polypharmacy, interacting co-morbidities, and genetic variations.

The prize is awarded annually to the most novel, important, and testable idea to reduce life threatening drug-drug, drug-disease, or pharmacogenomic interactions.

Emerald Lake Safety is proud to be the 2019 underwriter of the award and prize administration costs.

For more information, visit www.sternfelsprize.org.
